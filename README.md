# Zen Trading Tech — Public

**Simple daily practice rules for day traders.** Open data, indicators, and research to support traders in identifying and implementing their trading edge consistently — and in a healthy way.

🌐 [zentradingtech.com](https://zentradingtech.com)  ·  📺 [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/@zentradingtech)

## What's in this repo

📊 **[Data](./data)** — OHLC and indicator-tagged datasets for ES, FDAX, HSI, and Nikkei futures across multiple timeframes and sessions.

📈 **[Indicators](./indicators)** — Trading indicators and tools you can drop into your own research and execution workflow.

📚 **[Research](./research)** — Published quantitative studies organised by instrument. Probability-first, price-action grounded.

## About Zen Trading Tech

Zen Trading Tech is built around a simple idea: traders perform best when they have a clear edge, simple rules, and a healthy daily practice to execute them. Everything in this repo exists to support that.

For full research, courses, and the trading room: [zentradingtech.com](https://zentradingtech.com).
